Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is GreatUniHack?

    GreatUniHack is an event organised by the students from University of Manchester. It's aimed at students interested in technology who will join us for 36 hours, and turn their ideas into great hacks! It's a fantastic opportunity to meet new people, learn from mentors, and make cool stuff. They're very beginner friendly. Everybody is there to help each other and have fun. Check this out for more info.

  • Who can attend?

    GreatUniHack is open to all students 18 and older.

  • What if I'm not from Manchester?

    GreatUniHack is open to all students from whichever city or country you are. Our partner MLH, is providing us with the "first class" coaches for attendees from the UK. For those of you coming from outside of UK, unfortunately we are currently unable to provide you with any travel reimbursements.

  • I have graduated. Can I still attend?

    Maybe. This depends on you and when you graduated. If you graduated in the last 12 months, you're still considered a student. Any more than 12 months, you will need to email us as we deal with these on a case-by-case basis.

  • Can I build my hack before the event?

    No. It's unfair and immediately disqualifies you from presenting or winning any prizes. You can think of ideas, just don't start building them. You can of course use libraries, frameworks, and templates.

  • Do I need a team to participate?

    No. Your team can be between 1 and 4. Go solo if you wish.

  • I'm new to coding or can't code at all. Can I still come?

    The hackathon community welcomes you with open arms - plenty of mentors want to help you make your first hack a reality. We'll have mentors on site to teach you lots of new skills and guide you towards creating your first hack. All the students are very friendly and willing to help each other out.

  • Do you provide food and drinks?

    Yes, there will be plenty of food and drinks thanks to our sponsors and partners.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop, a phone and chargers. We'll provide you with everything else. Oh and also, you might want to bring a sleeping bag.

  • Would I be able to volunteer?

    Yes! If you would like to volunteer, fill out this form, and we'll be in touch. If you have any questions regarding volunteering, please send us an email to [email protected].

  • Who owns my IP?

    You own all the rights to your IP, we won't claim right over anything created during the event.

  • Do I need to be a programmer to attend this event?

    No. Even thought GreatUniHack is a tech oriented event, it is beginner friendly. So if you are a designer, an artist, a businessperson or whatever have you, you are welcome to join and hopefuly by the end of an event you will be a bit of a programmer as well :)
