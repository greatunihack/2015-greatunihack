The Winners

  • OculusDress and Side-Slash

    First place, Best use of BodyLabs API.

    See the meshes made using the BodyLabs API through Oculus. (+AR featuring a skeleton, pink bullets and fun)

  • Bloomopoly

    Runner-up, Best use of Bloomberg API

    Monopoly is more into trading than ever!

  • Ariel

    3rd Place

    Remarkably intelligent restricted Bolltzman machine playing piano. (tl;dr: it can learn how to play some Bach)

  • MLHorrors

    Crowd Favourite, Best use of Braintree_PayPal API

    ANOTHER Werewolf app... but different!

  • Retropolys

    2nd Crowd Favourite

    Go back to the golden age of n64 being the owner of everything!

  • EasyEmail

    Best use of Sendgrid API

    Receive your important emails when you don't have internet.

  • Aria

    Barclays challenge, Funniest Hack

    Login securely (heh) to online web services through the medium of classical Arias.

  • Love Letter

    Most Quirky Hack

    Send love letters to strangers.

  • Electronic Payment Parking Assistant

    Best use of Beacons

    Register when you arrie and leave a parking lot and charge you the correct amount when you leave.

  • TreeChart

    AmazonWebServices challenge

    A quick and easy way to discover everything you may need about different companies; plugin + website.

  • Dungeon Master

    Apadmi most retro challenge

    Retro Rogue-like dungeon crawler/designer.
